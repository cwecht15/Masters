Format

  • Each participant picks one player from each of six tiers based on the World Golf Rankings (as of 4/6/26).
  • Your best four scores out of six picks count towards your weekend total.
  • The two worst-performing picks on your roster are dropped automatically.

Scoring

  • Scores are standard stroke play relative to par for each round.
  • Players who miss the cut receive a score of 80 (+8 over par) for both Saturday and Sunday.
  • Your total is the sum of all four rounds for your best four golfers.

Winning

  • The participant with the lowest four-player total wins the pot.
  • Tiebreaker: The participant who has the highest-placing finisher on their roster wins. If still tied, work down the roster until someone has a higher finisher.
  • If still tied after the entire roster, the pot is split.
  • For tiebreaker purposes, all players who miss the cut count as the same finishing position.
